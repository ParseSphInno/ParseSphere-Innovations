Payment Terms for Sponsors or Contributors

  1. Nature of Sponsorship/Contribution:
    • Sponsors or contributors may provide financial support to ParseSphere Innovations purely out of goodwill or to support our mission. There is no obligation or expectation of specific benefits, rewards, or returns in exchange for their contributions.
  2. Currency of Payment:
    • Payments can be made in Indian Rupees (INR) or US Dollars (USD), depending on the preference of the sponsor or contributor.
  3. Payment Frequency:
    • Payments may be made as a one-time contribution or on a recurring basis, depending entirely on the sponsor's or contributor's discretion.
    • There is no fixed schedule for recurring payments; sponsors or contributors may make additional contributions at any time they choose, or they may choose to discontinue further payments after an initial contribution.
  4. Payment Process:
    • Contributions can be made via bank transfers, online payment gateways, or any other mutually agreed-upon method. The process will be outlined and agreed upon during the sponsorship or contribution discussion.
  5. Refund and Cancellation Policy:
    • Once a contribution is made, it is final and non-refundable, reflecting the voluntary nature of the support.
    • Sponsors or contributors are free to change their minds regarding future contributions without any penalties or obligations.
  6. Tax Implications:
    • Sponsors or contributors are responsible for understanding and managing any tax liabilities associated with their contributions, including potential deductions or declarations under applicable laws in their respective countries.
    • ParseSphere Innovations will provide necessary documentation, such as receipts, to support any tax reporting requirements.
  7. Legal Compliance:
    • All contributions must comply with relevant Indian laws, including the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA), 1999, for international contributions.
    • Sponsors or contributors must ensure that their payment methods are legal and comply with the regulations of their respective countries as well as Indian laws.
  8. Dispute Resolution:
    • Any disputes related to these payment terms will be resolved through mutual discussion. If an agreement cannot be reached, the dispute will be referred to arbitration in accordance with the Indian Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. The arbitration will take place in [Your City], India, and the language will be English.
  9. Modification of Payment Terms:
    • ParseSphere Innovations reserves the right to update or modify these payment terms as needed. Sponsors or contributors will be informed of any changes, and continued support will indicate acceptance of the updated terms.
